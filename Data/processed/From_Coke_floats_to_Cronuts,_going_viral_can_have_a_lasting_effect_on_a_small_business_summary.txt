**From Coke floats to Cronuts, going viral can have a lasting effect on a small business**  Viral
social media posts can dramatically increase small business demand. Savvy owners who adapt quickly
to the influx can leverage this fame into sustained success. Examples include the Lexington Candy
Shop's viral Coke float, Fatimas Grill's Flamin Hot Cheeto Fusion burger, and Aunt Bills' brisket
hot dog, which led to expansion and increased customer base.
