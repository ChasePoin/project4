**Private US Lander Destroyed During Reentry After Failed Mission to Moon, Company Says**
Astrobotic Technology's Peregrine lunar lander was destroyed upon atmospheric reentry after a fuel
leak prevented its moon landing. The company initiated the destruction to protect other spacecraft,
and an investigation will determine the cause of the malfunction. Despite the setback, Astrobotic
remains determined to continue lunar exploration with a larger lander planned for the future.
